Colors

We use color to indate the state of the app. Use English tenses as the metaphor, Primary corresponds to present tense; Action corresponds to future tense.

Primary:

Normal current state 996192
Light available options e0b7db
Dark ? ? ? 72486f

Background:

Normal ubiquitous fbfbfb
Light quietly waitting for input FFFFFF
Dark ? ? ? 3A3A3A

Text:

Default readable 3A3A3A
Annotation description/non-actionable 686868
Alert error/failure/danger EF2121
